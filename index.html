Pharaonic Civilization

It is a civilization that was prominent in the Mediterranean
nearly 30 centuries ago, in the period extending between (3100-332 BC).
The Pharaonic civilization was characterized by the prosperity of the fields
of art and beauty, which appeared clearly through architecture
in the construction of the great pyramids, and antiquities
and artifacts inscribed with hieroglyphs.
